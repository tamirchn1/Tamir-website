About

Music production, bass performance, and creative leadership in Berlin

This site is designed to help people find Tamir Chen for music production in Berlin, bass lessons in Berlin, podcast editing, live and studio bass sessions, and Ableton Live teaching. The goal is simple: make it easy to understand who he is, what he does, and how to work with him.

Tamir Chen is a music producer, bass player, and studio musician in Berlin. Originally from Tel Aviv and based in Berlin for over a decade, his work sits at the intersection of performance, recording, education, accessibility, and community building.

As a bass player for live and studio projects, he performs and records across genres including soul, jazz, bossa nova, psychedelic music, Mediterranean music, and experimental pop. He also records, produces, edits, mixes, and helps artists shape complete creative projects from first demo to final master.

Tamir is the founder of Solarium Studio in Neukoelln, an artistic hub for bands, rehearsal, events, recording, and the annual Solarium Festival. Alongside production work, he teaches bass guitar and Ableton Live in Berlin, facilitates bands through Bands Berlin, and works with children and people with special needs through music.
