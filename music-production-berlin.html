FAQ

Do you work only in Berlin?

No. Berlin is the base, but projects can also move through remote collaboration, editing, planning, and production support online.

Can you help if I only have demos?

Yes. Demo-stage work is often where strong production has the biggest impact, especially on structure, arrangement, and sonic identity.

Can I also book you as a player on the project?

Yes. If the project needs bass, session work, or broader musical input, that can be part of the same collaboration.
